Black mambas

The black mamba is one of the fastest and deadliest snakes in the world. Two drops of its venom can kill a person.

  • Scientific Name: Dendroaspis polylepis
  • Average Length: 2.5meters (4.6 ft)
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Habitat: Sub-Saharan Africa

The black mamba is a highly venomous snake endemic to parts of sub-Saharan Africa. Skin colour varies from grey to dark brown. Juvenile black mambas tend to be paler than adults and darken with age.
